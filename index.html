HELLO WORLD, THIS IS OVISHAKE

Raja or Ovishake, mis-pronounced as Avishek, from Pittsburgh, PA home to Pittsburgh Steelers.I work as a Research Software Development Engineer at Servicelink (a subsidiary of Fidelity National Financial) at Coraopolis, Moon Township, Pittsburgh located next to the Pittsburgh International Airport. I work mainly on Computer Vision problems as in Object Detection, Instance Segmentation, Video Analytics, sometimes Object Classification but its not that simple, I take out the logits convert it into probability and do a lot of stuff on that layer. I have strong fundamentals in pre-deep-learning algorithms like ORB (RIP SIFT SURF thanks to OpenCV being Open and not including anything proprietary), Corner Detection, Edge Detection and image clustering techniques (if you know Kolmogorov Equation or Hilbert's 13th equation -- showing the fact that I did actually study some math) you would know what I am talking about :-D. I prune and quantize deep models developed in Tensorflow or Pytorch before deploying them on cloud or on FPGAs. Apart from that I upscale/super-resolution images with GANs, or fix motion blurred images with GANs. I also work with Language Data in which I build various Text Classification, Text Generation, Text Summarization models, fine-tune existing models to our needs and Image Caption some images . I also take part in cleaning or extracting some of the texts using OCR (Teseract) and Information Retrieval using NER (mainly Spacy and sometimes NLTK) . Sometimes I find out semantic similarity between texts/sentences etc. I also build small statistical models (as they are smaller than Deep Learning models) like fitting data points to distributions or curves, some regression models or clustering/component analysis of various kinds. I worked on the engineering side as well where I deployed models to Azure cloud or Azure ML workspaces, and have inevitably dealt with Azure Queues and some NoSQL databases, Docker and Kubernetes. We all at our company build well-designed, scaleable code, designed to perfection. I benefit vastly by working with some of the best talents at our company, most of whom are Ivy-League grads and are exceptionally organized individuals. Prior to this I worked under Prof. Dr. Prabir Bhattacharya & Prof Dr. Siwei Lyu (now at SUNY Buffalo) where I apply AI, Computer Vision & Image Processing/Segmentation for Detection of Pathologies from Radiological Images. Prior to this I have some Software Engineering experience at Tata Consultancy Services, Larsen & Toubro Infotech Ltd.
